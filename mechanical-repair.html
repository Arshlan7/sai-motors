Mechanical Repair Services

At SAI MOTORS, we provide complete mechanical repair solutions to keep your vehicle running smoothly, safely, and efficiently.

Engine Diagnosis & Repair

We specialize in diagnosing and repairing engine issues to restore optimal performance.

  • Complete engine overhaul & rebuilding
  • Engine restoration for old vehicles
  • Timing belt & chain replacement
  • Head gasket repair
  • Piston, crankshaft & valve work
  • Oil leakage diagnosis & repair
  • Troubleshooting for noise/overheating

Suspension & Steering Work

A healthy suspension system ensures comfort, stability, and safe handling on the road.

  • Shock absorber & strut replacement
  • Coil spring repair and replacement
  • Control arm, bushings & ball joints
  • Wheel alignment support (pre/post work)
  • Steering rack & tie-rod repair
  • Suspension noise diagnosis

Brake System Repairs

Your safety is our priority. We ensure your braking system performs at its best.

  • Brake pad & disc replacement
  • Brake caliper repair
  • Brake fluid replacement & bleeding
  • Brake noise and vibration fixing
  • ABS-related mechanical checks

Transmission & Clutch Services

Smooth gear shifting improves performance and driving comfort.

  • Manual transmission repair
  • Clutch & pressure plate replacement
  • Release bearing replacement
  • Flywheel inspection
  • Gearbox noise diagnosis

Cooling System & Overheating Fixes

We prevent engine overheating and cooling failures.

  • Radiator repair & replacement
  • Coolant flush & refilling
  • Water pump replacement
  • Thermostat replacement
  • Cooling fan and hose inspection

General Mechanical Repairs

For day-to-day mechanical reliability.

  • Engine mounting replacement
  • Belt replacement (Serpentine & Accessory)
  • Exhaust system inspection & repair
  • Bearing and pulley replacement
  • Preventive mechanical inspections

Door to Door Service Available! 🏠

Pickup and drop-off available for all major repairs at your convenience.
